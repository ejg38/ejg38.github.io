Miami’s Arts District

The result of an artificial gentrification process

Gentrification refers to the arrival of wealthier and often younger people in an existing urban district, a related increase in rents and property values, and changes in the district’s character and culture. It tends to take lots of time for changes to happen, however in Wynwood things were quite different.

It took Tony Goldman, the late modern American developer, three years to finally be able to enjoy the life he brought into Wynwood, now Miami’s hippest and hottest emerging neighborhood.
